About

Art meets technology.
Experience meets emotion.

I'm Wave Pongruengkiat (วีรธัช พงษ์เรืองเกียรติ), a creative technologist based in Chiang Mai, Thailand. I create interactive installations where visitors become part of the art — their bodies, gestures, and presence drive real-time generative visuals and sound.

My work sits at the intersection of art, technology, and human experience. From motion-captured dance performances in Korea to biosensor-driven installations, I build systems where technology disappears and only the experience remains.
