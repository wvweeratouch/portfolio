Digital Identity

Every time we face a camera — surveillance, social media, video calls — our faces become data. Data Mask makes this process visible and tangible. Visitors stand before a screen, and their face is instantly mapped with 468 tracking points that become the foundation for generative digital masks.

The masks aren't pre-designed. They're generated in real-time from the unique geometry of each visitor's face. Your expressions, your micro-movements, your identity — all become raw material for a digital transformation.

The Experience

Visitors approach a screen and see their face overlaid with flowing, evolving digital patterns. The masks respond to expression: smiling triggers different generative behaviors than frowning. Moving closer or further changes the visual density and detail.

The piece operates at two levels: as an engaging, visually striking interactive experience (people love seeing themselves transformed), and as a subtle commentary on facial recognition and the commodification of identity data.

Face Mesh

468-point face tracking via MediaPipe. Sub-millimeter precision.

Expression Analysis

Smile, frown, surprise detection drives visual behavior changes.

Generative Masks

Procedural visual generation from facial geometry. Unique per face.

Real-time

60fps tracking and rendering. No latency between expression and response.
